About Us

we devoted ourselves for the service of food lovers.

P epperazi was founded in 2015 by few college students. Situated in Sector 62, Noida in the heart of a populated upper-middle-class neighbourhood, the concept was simply to create a feasible platform that was well suited to have great conversations, served quality freshly cooked meals and offered great pleasantries and spirit selections. We provide friendly, attentive service in a relaxed environment by staff and owners that truly enjoy their job. Over the years our menu has changed to reflect today's consumer. Our image is a reflection of today's consumer needs and the change in current styles.

O n this platform, you can not only place your orders, but also search recipes and tutorials of your favourite chefs and cook just like them. We have strong tie-ups with the major restaurants and we consider it to be our duty to provide top notch, high quality food in our service. We're on a mission to provide restaurant like environment to our customers sitting at home.
