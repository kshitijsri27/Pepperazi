About Our Chefs

Sanjeev Kapoor

A Famous Chef

Sanjeev is an Indian chef and entrepreneur. Kapoor stars in the TV show Khana Khazana, which is the longest running show of its kind in Asia; it broadcasts in 120 countries and in 2010 had more than 500 million viewers.

Born: April 10, 1964 (age 51), Ambala
Spouse: Alyona Kapoor (m. 1992)
Cooking style: Indian cuisine
TV shows: Khana Khazana, MasterChef India, Cook Smart
Children: Kriti Kapoor, Rachita Kapoor
Awards: ITA Award for Best Cookery Show, STAR Parivaar Award for Favourite Judge, ITA Award for Popular Chef & Entrepreneur.

Vikas Khanna

Master Chef

Vikas Khanna is an award winning Michelin starred Indian chef, restaurateur, and cookbook writer, filmmaker, humanitarian and the judge of the TV show MasterChef India, Season 3 and Masterchef Junior. He is based in New York City. .

Born: November 14, 1971 (age 43), Amritsar
Height: 1.84 m
Parents: Davinder Khanna, Bindu Khanna
Education: Cornell University, New York University, Welcomgroup Graduate School of Hotel Administration
TV shows: Junior Masterchef India, Coastal Curries

Master Chef
